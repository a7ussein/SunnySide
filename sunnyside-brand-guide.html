04
Logo Usage Rules

✓ Do

  • Use the logo on dark navy, white, cream, or gold backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the sun height around the logo
  • Keep the wordmark "Sunny" in gold italic, "side" in the contrasting color
  • Use the icon mark alone for favicons, app icons, and social profiles
  • Scale proportionally from all corners

✗ Don't

  • Don't place the logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't change the gold/navy color relationship
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or warp the logo
  • Don't add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • Don't rearrange the sun, house, and wordmark elements

Minimum Clear Space

Maintain minimum clear space of "X" (sun diameter) on all sides of the logo.

05
Brand Voice

Sunnyside's voice is warm, professional, and empowering. We speak with compassion, not condescension. We project confidence, not rigidity. Our words invite trust and convey genuine care.

Our voice sounds like

  • "We're here to help you live your best life at home."
  • "Your comfort, dignity, and independence matter to us."
  • "Together, we create a care plan that fits your life."
  • "Our certified caregivers bring warmth and expertise."

Our voice doesn't sound like

  • "We take care of elderly and disabled people."
  • "Our patients need our help with basic tasks."
  • "We service cases in the Lewiston area."
  • Clinical jargon without context or warmth.
