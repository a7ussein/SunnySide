About Us

Care That Feels Like Family

Sunnyside Home Care LLC provides personal care services to older residents and people living with disabilities throughout Maine. We hire and employ direct care personnel to provide assistance with Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) and Instrumental Activities of Daily Living (IADLs) in the places where our clients reside.

"Our mission is to provide compassionate, person-centered care in a safe, respectful, and nurturing environment."
What We Do

Personal Care Services

Comprehensive support tailored to each individual's needs, preferences, and goals — empowering clients to live independently and with dignity.

Activities of Daily Living

Hands-on personal care assistance to support daily independence and well-being.

  • Bathing & personal hygiene
  • Dressing assistance
  • Toileting & continence care
  • Mobility & transfer support
  • Eating assistance & clean-up
  • Health maintenance activities

Instrumental ADLs

Support with household and community tasks for a comfortable, organized life.

  • Meal preparation & clean-up
  • Grocery & medication shopping
  • Light housekeeping & laundry
  • Routine cleaning tasks
  • Household budgeting assistance

Person-Centered Planning

Individualized care plans developed with you, your family, and our team.

  • Comprehensive intake assessment
  • Custom service plan development
  • Regular plan reviews & updates
  • Family collaboration
  • Quality supervisory visits
Why Sunnyside

Built on Trust, Driven by Heart

01

Fully Licensed & Compliant

Licensed by Maine DHHS, HIPAA compliant, and fully insured. All staff undergo criminal background checks and complete rigorous training programs including CPR/First Aid certification.

02

Certified PSS Caregivers

Our Personal Support Specialists are certified, competency-tested, and receive ongoing professional development. Every caregiver adheres to a strict Code of Ethics.

03

Quality Assurance

Monthly reviews, quarterly supervisory visits, annual satisfaction surveys, and continuous performance improvement ensure the highest quality care for every client.

04

MaineCare & Insurance

We handle all MaineCare billing and coordination. Electronic Visit Verification (EVV) ensures accurate service documentation and seamless claims processing.
